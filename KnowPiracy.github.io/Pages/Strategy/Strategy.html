The Plan

There are two parts to getting what you want: discouragement and encouragement. One has to make choices to create a system that discourages some options and encourages others. Once you know what you want this becomes simple.


Discouragement

Here we discourage negative options by pressuring the current economic system with piracy. This means people need to get good at piracy. Specifically, piracy needs best-practices and diversification. Best practices ought include a lot, but most especially ways to learn how to pirate. Most pirated material is media. Academic and Physicals are lacking.


Encouragement

Here we encourage positive options by presenting economic and legal options. Economic options are more important, like much more important. Systems from crowd funding to tax-credits need to be presented and supported. Legal options are less important, but there are plenty of them in these: Constitutions IP Clause, First Amendment's Free Speech, UN's Human Rights, Political Pirate Parties, Religious Kopimism.
