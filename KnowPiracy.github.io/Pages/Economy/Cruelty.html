Mini-page: The Brutal System

To be clear, absolutely no one should be recommending a brutal system. In a brutal system economic players have the options of copying or creating. Winning is defined has having a greater profit than your opponents; consequently, the only way to win is to make a product with a higher quality per price ratio. The best way to do this is to copy your opponents, and work from there. End Result: In simple competition with cheap mimicry, the cost of creation cripples profits.
